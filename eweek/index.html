ENGINEERS' WEEK

UCLA | April 4 - 8 2016 | Powered by ESUC


Team Sign Up

Calendar

Monday, April 4th

MRS Liquid Nitrogen Ice Making (2:00-4:00 PM) — Engineering and culinary skills are put to the test as teams compete to make the best tasting liquid nitrogen made ice cream.

Theta Tau Dodgeball (4:00-6:00 PM) — In this physical test, teams will compete with each other in a classic game of dodgeball!

ACM Cryptography Contest (6:00-8:00 PM) — Teams will create their own encryption methods and other teams must attempt to encrypt each other’s messages. First team to decrypt another team’s message wins!

AIChE “The Martian” Screening (8:00-10:00 PM) — This movie screening is filled with trivia and potato themed prizes.

Tuesday, April 5th

UPE Coding Challenge (4:00-6:00 PM) — In an algorithm and logic based challenge, teams demonstrate their problem solving abilities through their code.

IEEE Gaming Tournament (6:00-9:00 PM) — IEEE presents the IEEE Eweek Super Smash Bros Tourney! Teams will compete in Nintendo’s most famous fighter game. Teams will have up to 5 members each in a 2 v. 2 or 4 v. 4 game.






Wednesday, April 6th

EAP Tour of the Birthplace of the Internet (10:00 AM-3:00 PM) — The Engineering Ambassador Program will be hosting tours of the room where the Internet first came to be.

AiChE Crossword Puzzles (10:30 AM-2:30 PM) — AiChE will be handing out crossword puzzles in the Court of Sciences for engineers to complete. Finish the crossword and return to redeem a prize!

ASCE Tower Building (4:00-6:00 PM) — The strongest, sturdiest, and highest built tower takes the trophy.

SWE Jeopardy (6:00-8:00 PM) — The Society of Women Engineers provides the answers from a variety of categories, with an emphasis on Equality in Engineering, in this Q&A style competition!

Thursday, April 7th

ESUC Egg Drop (12:00-1:30 PM) — Given a limited supply of materials and time, teams create capsules that will have to survive the 80 foot drop!

ASME Tug of War (2:30-4:00 PM) — Brute force won’t be the determining factor in this game of physics between teams!

ESUC Talent Show - (6:00-9:00 PM) — Students of the UCLA engineering community come together for a showcase of their creative talents, ranging from stand-up comedy to dances to musical performances.

Create. Connect. Celebrate.

About EWeek

This year, Engineer’s Week will have a competitive aspect to encourage student leadership and service, inspire student participation, provide opportunities for industry involvement, and foster a sense of teamwork and community. Students will have the opportunity to sign up for a team of 4-6 members. Students can also choose to be placed in a team.

Teams can earn points through participation throughout the week, such as by posting on social media, attending events, and participating in competitions. Specific rules are listed below. Prizes vary from gift cards to prizes sponsored by top tech companies. Specific prizes are also listed below. There will be prizes at the end of the week for the teams with the most points as well as prizes at events that involve competitions. Get ready for a week of meeting new people, making memories with friends, and winning great prizes!
